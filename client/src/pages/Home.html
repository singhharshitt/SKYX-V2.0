How It Works.

Experience seamless currency conversion with our intelligent platform. From input to visualization, every step is optimized for speed and accuracy.

01

Choose Conversion Mode

Users select between Fiat or Cryptocurrency conversion modes and enter the desired amount for instant processing.

02

Smart Input Processing

Vanilla JavaScript validates all inputs in real-time, ensuring data integrity before sending the conversion request.

03

Secure Backend Request

Data is securely transmitted to our Node.js + Express backend, ensuring protected and reliable communication.

04

Live Exchange Rates

Our backend fetches real-time rates from CoinGecko and ExchangeRate APIs, delivering up-to-the-second accuracy.

05

Instant Conversion Result

Accurate converted values are calculated and returned instantly, displayed in a clean, easy-to-read format.

06

Interactive Charts

Visualize 7-day price history with beautiful Chart.js graphs, helping you track trends and make informed decisions.

07

Global Visualization

A stunning 3D rotating globe powered by Three.js highlights our worldwide currency coverage and global reach.

08

Responsive Experience

Fully optimized for all screen sizes using TailwindCSS, ensuring a seamless experience on mobile, tablet, and desktop.
